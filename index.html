Hi, I'm Lynsey

Aspiring software developer

"Learn as much as you can about as much as you can" - Tim Minchin

Having landed in the IT world in 2017, I spent the next 6.5 years working as a Project Manager/Scrum Master (and sometimes Product Owner). After redundancies and contract work, I took 6 months off to explore the Asia-Pacific.

During my trip I decided I wanted to expand my T-shaped skills. Having spent countless hours working closely with developers, and finding it super inspiring that they could build really cool things, I decided I should get on the bandwagon and learn to code too. I was lucky enough to join a 12-week bootcamp with io.Academy. Check out some of the neat things I've made during the course.

Outside of work I can be found hiking a mountain, admiring the beauty of nature (I'm in love with Japanese gardens), running a parkrun, playing card games or enjoying a cheeky pint.
